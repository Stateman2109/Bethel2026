Welcome Message

A special message from the couple

Dear Family & Friends

Welcome to Bethel 2026—a sacred celebration of love, faith, and divine covenant.

As you join us on this special occasion, we warmly welcome you to witness the union between Adeolu Johnson and Omokehinde Esther taking place on October 31st, 2026 at St. Matthias Anglican Church, where two hearts come together before God to begin a lifelong journey of love, purpose, and commitment.

The theme of this wedding, “Bethel”, meaning “House of God” or “The Dwelling Place of God,” reflects the spiritual foundation upon which this union is built. Our desire is to establish a home centered on Christ, a home filled with love, prayer, peace, faithfulness, and the presence of God.

We believe that marriage is a divine institution ordained by God, and this gathering marks the beginning of a Godly legacy rooted in biblical values and unconditional love. As family, friends, and loved ones, your presence, prayers, and support mean so much to us as we dedicate this new chapter to God’s glory.

May this celebration inspire hearts, strengthen faith, and remind us all that a home built on God can stand through every season of life.

Thank you for being part of Bethel 2026. May the Lord bless you richly as you celebrate with us.

Adeolu & Kehinde
